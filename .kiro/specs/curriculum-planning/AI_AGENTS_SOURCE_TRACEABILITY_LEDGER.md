# AI Agents Source Map and Traceability Ledger

Last reconciled: 2026-06-09

This ledger is the planning gate for book-derived AI agents curriculum changes. It reconciles the local `books/` folder, the existing private RAG corpus, and the active Course 1 repo surface before any learner-facing lesson is edited.

The ledger does not replace `ROADMAP.md`, `SPEC.md`, `CURRICULUM_REVIEW.md`, `LEARNER_READY_MATRIX.md`, or `curriculum/LEARNER_JOURNEY_MAP.md`. It exists to keep agent-related improvements evidence-led, scoped to stable folders, and small enough for review.

## Evidence Rules

- Use `.kiro/specs/curriculum-planning/master_ai_engineering_rag_corpus/sources.json` as the indexed-source baseline.
- Use `books/` as the local-file inventory baseline.
- Do not quote or redistribute extracted book text in this repo.
- Do not cite a book for a lesson claim until a future author retrieves chapter, section, page, location, or chunk evidence.
- Mark unindexed sources as `Needs Human Review` for source claims beyond title, filename, format, and intended curriculum role.
- Treat `Business_Aql_Marketing_Strategy_Plan.pdf` as private/sensitive optional context, not generic public curriculum evidence.
- Route every concept before accepting it: `Course 1 required`, `Course 1 preview`, `Course 2 bridge`, `Course 3 advanced`, `Specialization`, or `Parked`.
