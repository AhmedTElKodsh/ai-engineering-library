# Hints

## Hint 1

A golden example should include the query, expected abstention flag, expected citation requirement, and safety category.

## Hint 2

Keep the evaluator deterministic. Use fake observed outputs rather than live model calls.

## Hint 3

Score one requirement at a time: abstention, citation presence, and safety label.

## Hint 4

The report should name failure categories so a teammate knows what regressed.
